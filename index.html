About Me

Hey! My name is Patrick and I am a Developer residing in Chicago, IL. I enjoy creating aesthetically pleasing websites from visual design to back-end. I was in pension administration and finance in a past life and one day I realized I needed to find a career that would allow me to be creative.

In my quest to start my career in development, I attended a full-time programming boot-camp at Coding Temple, a private organization located in Chicago aimed at teaching Full-Stack Development in the .Net Framework and other related technologies. Upon completion, I gained professional experience working on short software sprint building an application using the .NET framework, SQL Server, and AngularJS. I'm excited continue developing my skills and to find opportunities that will help me expand my knowledge!

Some of my hobbies include playing basketball, reading, traveling, and photography. Check out a few of my pictures below!
